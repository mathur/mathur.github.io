About Me

A little blast from the past.

I was born on December 23rd, 1995, in Bridgewater, NJ. I had the privilege of gaining a great education in my schooling, building up to higher level classes in later years. Soon, I received my first video game console, a Playstation Portable. After getting bored with playing video games, I decided to try my hand at making my own games, using Actionscript (Adobe Flash), which could be played via the console's web browser.

My love of programming has led me to learn more languages and technologies. I currently attend the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, pursing a Computer Engineering and Business Management double major.
